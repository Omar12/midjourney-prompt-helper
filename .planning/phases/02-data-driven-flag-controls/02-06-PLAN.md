    Plans 01-05 implemented: the complete flag controls feature.   </what-built>

  <how-to-verify>
    Run the app: npm run dev, open http://localhost:5173 in your browser.

    1. SECTION VISIBLE: Confirm a "Flags" section heading appears below the chip area in the left pane. All 6 control groups should be visible (Version, Aspect Ratio, Stylize, Chaos, Seed, Exclude).

    2. VERSION SELECTOR:
       - Open the Version dropdown. Confirm options: V8.1 (Latest), V7, V6.1, Niji 7, Niji 6 (Legacy) — in that order.
       - Select V7. Confirm the live preview does NOT yet show a flag (no flags are set yet).
       - Click the × next to Version. Confirm the placeholder "MJ default (no flag)" reappears and version clears.

    3. ASPECT RATIO — PRESETS:
       - Click the "16:9" preset chip. Confirm it highlights (filled/default variant).
       - Confirm the live preview now ends with "--ar 16:9".
       - Click "1:1". Confirm 16:9 deselects and 1:1 highlights. Preview shows "--ar 1:1".
       - Click the × button next to Aspect Ratio. Confirm AR clears from preview.

    4. ASPECT RATIO — CUSTOM INPUT:
       - Type "21:9" in the custom input field. Press Enter. Confirm preview shows "--ar 21:9".
       - Type "bad:input" and press Enter. Confirm a validation error appears ("Use W:H format") and preview is NOT updated.
       - Type "16:9 --stylize 999" and press Enter. Confirm validation error and NO injection in preview.

    5. STYLIZE (SLIDER):
       - Toggle the Stylize enable switch to ON. Confirm the slider becomes active and the preview shows "--stylize" with some value.
       - Drag the slider or use arrow keys to set 250. Confirm preview shows "--stylize 250".
       - Toggle the switch back OFF. Confirm "--stylize" disappears from the preview.
       - [ASSUMED A1] Confirm range 0-1000 matches your Midjourney experience.

    6. CHAOS (SLIDER):
       - Toggle Chaos ON. Adjust to 30. Confirm preview shows "--chaos 30".
       - [ASSUMED A2] Confirm range 0-100 matches your Midjourney experience.

    7. SEED (NUMBER FIELD):
       - Type 12345 in the Seed field. Confirm preview shows "--seed 12345".
       - Confirm the × button appears and clicking it clears the seed from preview.
       - [ASSUMED A3] Confirm max 4294967295 is the correct seed ceiling in MJ.

    8. EXCLUDE (--no):
       - Type "trees, watermark" in the Exclude field. Tab away (blur). Confirm preview shows "--no trees, watermark".
       - Type "--ar 16:9" in the Exclude field and tab away. Confirm the output shows "--no -ar 16:9" (sanitized, injection neutralized) and NOT "--ar 16:9" as a separate flag.
       - Click × to clear. Confirm --no disappears from preview.

    9. COMBINED PROMPT:
       - Set intent "a cat". Add chip "cinematic". Set version V7. Set AR 16:9. Enable Stylize 250.
       - Confirm live preview reads: "a cat, cinematic --v 7 --ar 16:9 --stylize 250"
       - Copy the prompt (copy button). Paste into Midjourney /imagine. Confirm MJ accepts the syntax.

    10. VERSION GATING:
        - Set some flags (AR, Stylize, Chaos). Then switch version. Confirm all flags that support the new version still appear and retain their values.
        - [ASSUMED A4-A9] Confirm version labels match current MJ model availability.

    11. CLEAR:
        - Set version, AR, and Stylize. Click "Clear all". Confirm dialog says "This will clear your intent, all chips, and all flags." Click "Clear everything". Confirm all flag controls reset to unset and the preview is empty.

    12. RESPONSIVE:
        - Resize browser to narrow viewport (< 768px). Confirm the flag section stacks with the rest of the left pane correctly.
  </how-to-verify>
